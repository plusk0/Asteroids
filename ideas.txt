=== WEAPON IDEAS ===

1. **Black Hole Generator**
   - Creates a temporary black hole that pulls in nearby asteroids
   - Upgrades: 
     - Size/range of black hole
     - Duration
     - Pull strength
     - Chance to instantly destroy asteroids that get too close

=== FINAL UPGRADES SYSTEM ===

Final upgrades are special upgrades that become available when a base weapon reaches level 3.
These upgrades REPLACE the base weapon with a more powerful variant.

Implemented:
- Laser -> Shockwave (at level 3)
  - Shockwave: Fires a pulse that damages all asteroids in a line without stopping
  - Visual: Red wave expanding outward with multiple lines (like magnetizer)

Planned Final Upgrades:
- Rotator -> Rotator Pro
  - Additional rotating orbitals that can fire independently
  - More complex firing patterns
  - Visual: Multiple concentric rings of orbitals

- EMP -> Super EMP
  - Larger radius with multiple pulse waves
  - Slows asteroids in addition to damaging
  - Visual: Massive expanding energy field

- Wingmen -> Elite Squadron
  - More wingmen with better AI
  - Multiple formations available simultaneously
  - Visual: Distinct wingman types with different colors

- Magnetizer -> Gravity Manipulator
  - Can switch between push and pull modes
  - Affects multiple asteroids with chain reactions
  - Visual: Swirling gravity field

Conditions for Unlocking:
- Primary: Base weapon reaches level 3
- Alternative conditions:
  - Weapon level + another stat (e.g., laser level 2 + piercing level 3)
  - Multiple weapons at certain levels
  - Specific achievements

=== IMPLEMENTATION NOTES ===

Screen Zoom:
- Implemented as 1 / (1 + 0.1 * (level - 1)) scale
- Centered on screen with black bars at edges
- Gives impression of zooming out as player progresses

Wingmen:
- Size: 1/2 of player radius
- Speed: 100 (half of base shot speed 200)
- Start at level 0, first upgrade gives 1 wingman
- Every 5 levels adds another wingman (up to 6)
- Formations unlocked via "Wingman Formations" one-time upgrade
- Role distribution unlocked via "Basic Fighter Maneuvers" one-time upgrade

Magnetizer:
- Base level 0: no effect
- Push range: 75 (half of original 150)
- Grey color with rolling expanding circle effect
- Resistance: Tier 1 = 1.0, Tier 2 = 0.5, Tier 3 = 0.25
- At level 5: push_amount = 200, sufficient to keep medium asteroids away

Difficulty:
- Easy: 1.0 speed multiplier, no tier 3 asteroids
- Medium: 1.5 speed multiplier, tier 1-2 asteroids only
- Nightmare: 1.8 speed multiplier, all tiers including tier 3

Final Upgrades:
- Unlocked when base weapon reaches level 3
- Replaces base weapon in weapon list
- Shockwave replaces laser with line-based damage
